About

This rustic farm accommodation is situated in the hills of the Blue Mountains. The owner, Marina Ye, a local interior designer purchased the old rundown barn three years ago with a plan to renovate. After some elbow grease and a few late nights, the property has been restored to its former glory. This luxurious barn house is home to sheep, chickens, ducks and one very friendly goat named Rosie. There is an outdoor plunge pool just off the back verandah, and a stunning cottage garden.

This lovely bedroom was recently renovated, this is a beautiful and rustic style room with a king sixze bed. there is also more rooms available for other guests you might bring along. See "more info' to find out about the other bedrooms avaiable in the house.
